## NOTICE FOR SPECIAL GENERAL MEETING (SGM)

Notice is hereby given that a **Special General Meeting (SGM)** of the Association of Apartment Owners of '**Sobha Jasmine Condominium**' shall be held on **15th February 2026 (Sunday)** at **3 PM** within the society premises, to place before the Apartment Owners certain foundational governance matters arising from the property's submission to the Karnataka Apartment Ownership Act (KAOA), 1972, and to regularize and strengthen the statutory governance framework of the condominium in conformity with the said Act and the Deed of Declaration (DoD), and to consider and pass the resolutions set out hereinbelow:

---

## AGENDA:

**1.** To formally reaffirm that the Association of Apartment Owners of **Sobha Jasmine Condominium** stands governed exclusively under KAOA 1972, and the registered DoD of 2006, and not under any other statutory framework.

**2.** To approve and adopt a Supplementary Deed as outlined in paragraph 18 of the DoD for the limited purpose of enabling workable, KAOA-compliant governance and lawful amendment of Bye-laws, and effective functioning of the statutory Board of Office Bearers.

**3.** To adopt revised Bye-laws framed in strict conformity with Section 16 of the KAOA, 1972.

**4.** To mandate the execution, registration and implementation of Deeds of Apartment in accordance with Sections 9(2), 12 and 14 of the KAOA, 1972, by all remaining 65 plus apartment owners, as an essential step towards title regularization, statutory compliance, and clarity of Undivided Share (UDS).

**5.** To approve and incorporate a mandatory "NOC-linked compliance clause" in the Deed of Apartment, stipulating that execution and registration of the Deed of Apartment shall be a mandatory pre-condition for issuance of any No-Objection Certificate (NOC) by the Board of Office Bearers for any future sale, transfer or conveyance of an apartment.

**6.** To authorize the Association of Apartment Owners of Sobha Jasmine Condominium, acting through its duly constituted Board of Office Bearers, to receive, at the appropriate stage and upon due compliance with law, all funds, assets, records and documents presently held by the welfare association registered under the Karnataka Societies Registration Act, 1960, pursuant to a separate and lawfully convened meeting under that framework.

---

This SGM shall be confined exclusively to matters of governance under the KAOA 1972, and shall not address the dissolution of any welfare association under the Karnataka Societies Registration Act (KSRA) 1960, which will be considered in a separate SGM, strictly in accordance with the law.

Copies of the proposed resolutions, the draft Supplementary Deed, the revised Bye-laws, and the explanatory notes shall be available for inspection by the Apartment Owners during the meeting.
