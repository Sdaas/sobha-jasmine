# RESOLUTIONS OF THE SGM HELD ON 15.02.2026

The Special General Meeting (SGM) of the Association of Apartment Owners of '**Sobha Jasmine Condominium**' (hereinafter, also referred to as the 'Association') was convened on **15th February 2026** at **3pm** at the Clubhouse within the society premises, pursuant to the Notice dated **7th February 2026**, to consider certain foundational governance matters arising from the project's submission to the Karnataka Apartment Ownership Act (KAOA) 1972, to regularize and strengthen the statutory governance framework of the Association in conformity with the said Act and the registered Deed of Declaration (DoD) of 2006, and to consider and pass the resolutions set out in the said Notice.

The President wished to provide more time for maximum participation by Owners for the Special General Meeting at 3pm and hence the meeting was adjourned by 30 mins. Post-adjournment, the President noted that while the Quorum for a Special General Meeting was present, the specific statutory quorum (75%) required for finalizing the Amendment to the Deed of Declaration was not physically present. Consequently, the House proceeded to consider the agenda for 'In-Principle Approval' and to authorize a Consent Collection Drive to secure the necessary mandate.

The President, Secretary, Joint-Secretary, Treasurer, Joint-Treasurer and Other Board of Managers members occupied the dais for conducting the meeting. The proceedings of the meeting were conducted as per Agenda.

The President welcomed all the members of the Apartment and subsequent to his welcome address, the following agenda were discussed and relevant resolutions were approved in-principle and authorized for circulation thereof in the said meeting:

1. To formally reaffirm that the Association of Apartment Owners of '**Sobha Jasmine Condominium**' stands governed exclusively under the KAOA, 1972 as per the registered DoD of 2006;
2. To approve the text of a Supplementary Deed amending the DoD for the limited purpose of enabling workable, KAOA-compliant governance and lawful amendment of Bye-laws;
3. To adopt revised Bye-laws framed in conformity with Section 16 of the KAOA, 1972;
4. To mandate the use and implementation of Deeds of Apartment in accordance with Sections 5(2) and 12 of the Act for present and future conveyances and title regularization; and
5. To authorize the Association of Apartment Owners of '**Sobha Jasmine Condominium**' to receive, at the appropriate stage and upon due compliance with law, all funds, assets, records and documents presently held by the Welfare Association registered under the Karnataka Societies Registration Act (KSRA), 1960.

After detailed discussion, all the members present unanimously confirmed and approved the agenda and in light of the same, the following resolutions were unanimously passed at the meeting:

---

## A. Resolution 1: Reaffirmation of Governance under KAOA, 1972

"RESOLVED THAT, the Association of Apartment Owners of '**Sobha Jasmine Condominium**' do hereby formally reaffirm and place on record that the apartment owners and the Association, in respect of Sobha Jasmine Condominium, stand governed exclusively by the provisions of the Karnataka Apartment Ownership Act, 1972 ('KAOA'), in terms of the registered Deed of Declaration dated 4 December 2006 ('DoD'), and that the condominium shall henceforth be administered strictly in accordance with KAOA, the DoD and the bye-laws framed thereunder."

"RESOLVED FURTHER THAT, all apartment owners, occupants, the Board of Managers and all other stakeholders shall be bound by KAOA 1972 and the DoD, and any parallel or inconsistent governance of the common areas and facilities through any entity registered under the Karnataka Societies Registration Act, 1960 ('KSRA') or otherwise shall stand expressly disapproved, and is to be sunset strictly in accordance with law through a separate KSRA SGM, to be held shortly."

"RESOLVED THAT the Board is authorized to circulate these approved drafts to all absent owners and collect their written consent to meet the 75% threshold required by the DoD."

- Proposed By: The President
- Seconded By: All the members of the Board of Managers

---

## B. Resolution 2: Approval of Supplementary Deed Amending Paragraph 18 of the DoD

"RESOLVED THAT, pursuant to Sections 11 and 16 of the Karnataka Apartment Ownership Act, 1972 ('KAOA'), read with the registered Deed of Declaration, and for the limited purpose of enabling workable, KAOA-compliant governance and consequential amendment of the bye-laws, the text of the draft Supplementary Deed proposing amendments to the Deed of Declaration, as placed before this Meeting and authenticated by the President for the purpose of identification, be and is hereby approved in principle."

"RESOLVED FURTHER THAT, the said Supplementary Deed shall:

a) provide a clear, definite and workable mechanism for amendment of the bye-laws and such limited and relevant provisions of the Deed of Declaration as may be permissible in law, by a defined majority of apartment owners calculated on the basis of their respective percentage of undivided interest;
b) recognize and permit electronic, hybrid and remote modes of obtaining consent of apartment owners, subject to appropriate verification standards, record-keeping requirements and audit trails, in a manner consistent with applicable law; and
c) remain strictly confined to governance, administrative and procedural aspects, and shall not in any manner alter, dilute or affect the percentage of undivided interest (UDS) of any apartment owner."

"RESOLVED ALSO THAT, subject to approval of the Supplementary Deed by the requisite majority of apartment owners in accordance with the Karnataka Apartment Ownership Act, 1972 and the Deed of Declaration, the Board of Managers of the Association of Apartment Owners of '**Sobha Jasmine Condominium**' be and is hereby authorized to finalize the text, execute the Supplementary Deed on behalf of the Association, and cause the registration thereof with the jurisdictional Sub-Registrar, and thereafter to file the same with the Competent Authority under KAOA, together with such forms, statements and annexures as may be required under applicable law."

"RESOLVED ALSO THAT, the Association of Apartment Owners of 'Sobha Jasmine Condominium' do hereby accord its in-principle approval to the proposed amendments to the Deed of Declaration by way of the Supplementary Deed, and to the adoption of the revised Bye-laws framed in conformity with the Karnataka Apartment Ownership Act, 1972."

"RESOLVED FURTHER THAT, in view of the statutory requirement of approval by not less than seventy-five per cent (75%) of the apartment owners, calculated on the basis of their respective percentage of undivided interest, the Board of Managers is hereby expressly authorized to circulate the finalized drafts of the Supplementary Deed and the revised Bye-laws to all apartment owners and to collect individual written consents and/or signatures, whether in physical or electronic form, in accordance with law."

"RESOLVED ALSO THAT, upon securing the requisite statutory majority, the Board of Managers shall be fully empowered to execute and register the Supplementary Deed, and to file, register and bring into force the revised Bye-laws with the Competent Authority under the Karnataka Apartment Ownership Act, 1972, without the need for any further SGM."

- Proposed By: The President
- Seconded By: All the members of the Board of Managers

---

## C. Resolution 3: Adoption of Revised Bye-laws under Section 16 KAOA

"RESOLVED THAT, the draft revised Bye-laws of the Association of Apartment Owners of '**Sobha Jasmine Condominium**', framed strictly in conformity with Section 16 of the Karnataka Apartment Ownership Act, 1972 ('KAOA') and in accordance with the registered Deed of Declaration, as placed before this Meeting and authenticated by the Chairperson for the purpose of identification, be and are hereby adopted as the Bye-laws of the Association of Apartment Owners of 'Sobha Jasmine Condominium', with effect from the date on which the corresponding amendment to the Deed of Declaration by way of the Supplementary Deed is duly executed, registered and filed with the Competent Authority under KAOA 1972."

"RESOLVED FURTHER THAT, the revised Bye-laws shall, inter alia:

a) align their terminology, structure and operative provisions strictly with the Karnataka Apartment Ownership Act, 1972 and the Deed of Declaration;
b) provide that voting rights of apartment owners shall be exercised strictly in proportion to the percentage of undivided interest appurtenant to each apartment;
c) set out clear, exhaustive and unambiguous provisions governing the composition, powers, functions, election, tenure and removal of the Board of Managers, consistent with KAOA and the Deed of Declaration;
d) specify the percentage of votes and procedural requirements for amendment of the Bye-laws, in strict conformity with Section 16(2)(k) of KAOA and the amended Paragraph 18 of the Deed of Declaration; and
e) incorporate enabling provisions for digital record-keeping, electronic issuance of notices, and electronic, hybrid or remote participation and voting, subject to verifiable authentication, audit trails and compliance with applicable law."

"RESOLVED ALSO THAT, subject always to the Karnataka Apartment Ownership Act, 1972 and the Deed of Declaration, the Board of Managers of the Association of Apartment Owners of '**Sobha Jasmine Condominium**' be and is hereby authorized to carry out only such minor, clerical or non-substantive modifications to the revised Bye-laws as may be required by the Competent Authority or advised by legal counsel for the purpose of compliance, and to execute, submit and file all necessary documents, forms and annexures to give effect to the adoption and registration of the revised Bye-laws, provided that no such modification shall alter any substantive right, voting entitlement or undivided interest of any apartment owner."

- Proposed By: The President
- Seconded By: All the members of the Board of Managers

---

## D. Resolution 4: Mandate for Deeds of Apartment and Title Regularization

"RESOLVED THAT, in order to give effect to and ensure compliance with Sections 5(2) and 12 of the Karnataka Apartment Ownership Act, 1972 ('KAOA'), and to secure title regularization and accuracy of undivided interest (UDS) for present and future conveyances, the Association of Apartment Owners of '**Sobha Jasmine Condominium**' do hereby adopt and mandate the systematic use and implementation of Deeds of Apartment ('DoA') in respect of apartments in Sobha Jasmine Condominium, in accordance with the registered Deed of Declaration, KAOA and the approved formats placed before this Meeting, for all future sales, transfers and conveyances, and for existing apartments to the extent permissible in law and with the consent of the concerned apartment owners."

"RESOLVED FURTHER THAT, subject to the provisions of the Karnataka Apartment Ownership Act, 1972 and the Deed of Declaration, the Board of Managers of the Association of Apartment Owners of '**Sobha Jasmine Condominium**' is hereby authorized to:

a) undertake a comprehensive UDS and title reconciliation exercise by comparing the undivided interest specified in the Deed of Declaration with that reflected in individual sale deeds and other title documents, solely for the purpose of identifying discrepancies, if any;
b) prepare apartment-wise Deeds of Apartment and ancillary statutory forms, including Form B, strictly in accordance with the Deed of Declaration, KAOA and the approved formats, for execution by the respective apartment owners; and
c) facilitate and coordinate the execution and registration of Deeds of Apartment in a structured and phased manner, with due notice to apartment owners, so as to minimize inconvenience while ensuring statutory compliance, transparency and long-term title clarity."

Provided that nothing herein shall be construed as authorizing the Board of Managers to unilaterally alter, vary or correct the undivided interest or title of any apartment owner without due process and the express consent of the concerned apartment owner, except as may be directed by a competent authority or court of law.

- Proposed By: The President
- Seconded By: All the members of the Board of Managers

---

## E. Resolution 5: Authorization to Receive Assets, Funds and Records from KSRA Welfare Association

"RESOLVED THAT, subject to and conditional upon the society registered under the Karnataka Societies Registration Act, 1960 ('KSRA') in the name of the Sobha Jasmine Condominium welfare association duly passing, in a separately convened Special General Meeting, valid resolutions for the transfer and vesting of its funds, assets and records and for its dissolution in accordance with Section 22 of the KSRA, and upon completion of all statutory formalities thereunder, the Association of Apartment Owners of '**Sobha Jasmine Condominium**' do hereby consent to and authorize the receipt of such funds, assets, records, contracts, books of account and documents as relate exclusively to the common areas, facilities and administration of Sobha Jasmine Condominium."

"RESOLVED FURTHER THAT, subject to the Karnataka Apartment Ownership Act, 1972, the Karnataka Societies Registration Act, 1960, and the resolutions duly passed by the KSRA-registered welfare association, the Board of Managers of the Association of Apartment Owners of 'Sobha Jasmine Condominium' be and is hereby authorized to:

a) Execute, receive and acknowledge handover statements, inventories, statements of accounts and transfer documents evidencing the lawful transfer of funds, assets and records from the KSRA-registered welfare association;
b) Operate, take-over or, where necessary, open bank accounts in the name of the Association of Apartment Owners of '**Sobha Jasmine Condominium**' solely for the purpose of consolidating and managing the Association funds received pursuant to such transfer, in accordance with applicable banking and statutory requirements; and
c) ensure that the migration and consolidation of funds, assets and records is fully documented, transparent, auditable and carried out strictly in conformity with applicable law and the resolutions passed by both entities."

Provided that nothing herein shall be construed as an assumption by the Association of Apartment Owners of '**Sobha Jasmine Condominium**' of any liabilities, obligations or commitments of the KSRA-registered welfare association other than those expressly transferred pursuant to law and duly approved resolutions.

- Proposed By: The President
- Seconded By: All the members of the Board of Managers

---

It is further noted and recorded that the present exercise undertaken through this Special General Meeting is confined to the rectification and regularization of statutory governance of Sobha Jasmine Condominium under the Karnataka Apartment Ownership Act, 1972, and that, having regard to the paucity of time, the multiplicity of compliance steps involved, and the objective of ensuring a legally compliant yet least burdensome transition for the apartment owners, it was considered neither necessary nor expedient to conduct fresh elections at this stage.

It was accordingly noted and agreed that, for the limited and transitional purpose of implementing the resolutions passed at this Meeting, including execution and registration of the Supplementary Deed, adoption and filing of the revised Bye-laws, and completion of consequential statutory compliances under KAOA, the existing Board of Managers, by whatever name called, and office bearers shall continue in office and be deemed duly authorized to exercise all powers and perform all acts required in that regard. This continuation shall operate strictly as a one-time, necessity-driven measure, shall not be treated as a precedent or dilution of democratic governance, and upon completion of the aforesaid transition, the Association shall convene one consolidated fresh election for a Board of Managers to function thereafter under the KAOA-compliant framework.

The President, on behalf of the Board of Managers of Association of Apartment Owners of '**Sobha Jasmine Condominium**', expressed vote of thanks to all members and sought their continued co-operation for smooth functioning of the Board.

As there was no other agenda, item or matter to be discussed, the meeting concluded with a vote of thanks.

Yours Sincerely,
